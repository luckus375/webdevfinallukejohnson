Student, Artist, Producer. About Luke Johnson

Music has always been a big part of my life. Ever since I was a kid, I have always had a song stuck in my head. Now that I am older, I make my own music, bringing the tunes in my head out into the real world. There's lots of artists who have had an influence on my productions. Here's my top ten musical influences:

  1. Juice Wrld
  2. Travis Scott
  3. Drake
  4. The Kid LAROI
  5. Don Toliver
  6. Kendrick Lamar
  7. Kanye West
  8. Weezer
  9. Green Day
  10. Baby Keem

Each of these artists have had an immense impact on my music. I started releasing original projects in October of 2020, and have enjoyed producing music ever since.
